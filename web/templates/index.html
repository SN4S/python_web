Our Philosophy

At c0vid bat, we serve a selection of the most unusual and exotic Asian cuisine you’ll find anywhere. Our passion for rare ingredients and bold flavors is what sets us apart. Each dish is thoughtfully crafted using ingredients that are often hard to come by and sourced from distant regions, including China.

Due to the rarity of our ingredients, the availability of our dishes changes frequently. We believe in giving our guests a fresh and exclusive experience with every visit. Some of our dishes may not be available after just one order, as certain ingredients are limited in quantity but shared among several creations.

Reserve Your Experience

To ensure you don’t miss out on your preferred dish, we allow advance bookings up to two months ahead. This way, you can enjoy the meal of your choice, even if it requires time to bring the necessary ingredients to our kitchen.

At c0vid bat, we don’t just offer a meal, we offer an experience—a daring exploration of tastes and sensations that can’t be found anywhere else.
